Rules

1. When you click "Start", a 3-second countdown will begin, at the end, your word will pop up for 1 second.

2. Spell the word as it appeared. If you spelled the word correctly, you get 1 point & a giphy.

3. If you spelled it wrong, you will get a point taken away.

4. When you are done, simply click "End Game"
